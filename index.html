Pet DNA test FAQ

Are pet DNA tests “100% accurate”?

No. Results are statistical and depend on the provider’s reference database, sampling quality, and how mixed the pet is. The best tests are usually those with the largest and most curated databases.

Does a “health risk” mean my pet is sick?

No. A risk marker is not a diagnosis. Use results as a discussion starter with a veterinarian.

Should I buy breed-only or breed + health?

If you mainly want “what mix is my dog?”, breed-first is fine. If you want actionable screening, choose a kit explicitly designed for health markers.
